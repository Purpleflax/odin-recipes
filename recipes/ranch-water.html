Texas Ranch Water

Description

Created by a Texas rancher as a way to beat the heat, this simple cocktail is mixed right in the bottle - perfect for cookouts or any outdoor activity. Use Topo Chico for the most authentic flavor and bubbly taste. Make sure your mineral water is ice cold for best results!

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle of sparking mineral water
  • 2 fluid ounces blanco tequila
  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice
  • lime wedge, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Drink about 3 ounces of mineral water out of the bottle. Slowly pour in tequila and lime juice. Place thumb over top of bottle, and gently turn upside-down and back up to combine.
  2. Serve with a lime wedge in the opening of the bottle, if desired.
